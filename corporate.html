The program

Two weeks. Full days. No fluff.

Mon-Fri. On-site at your office or in Austin.

Week 1 · Learn

How AI works. Applied to your work.

  • How AI models work (the intuition, not the math)
  • Prompt engineering for real business tasks
  • AI applied to your team's actual daily work
  • Identify automation opportunities across the org
  • Scope the project your team will build in Week 2
End of Week 1: Every participant has integrated AI into their actual job. Project scope approved by leadership.
Week 2 · Build

Build it. Ship it. Present it.

  • Full build sprint on the scoped project
  • No-code and low-code AI tools
  • Connect to your real data and systems
  • Test, refine, document for maintenance
  • Present the deployed tool to leadership
End of Week 2: A deployed, working AI tool. Presented live to leadership. Ready to use on Day 11.
