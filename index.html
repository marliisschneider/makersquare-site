Questions

Things people
usually ask us.

Do I need to know how to code?
No. The only prerequisite is basic computer fluency and real experience in your field. If you've spent years building expertise in your industry, that's exactly what we're looking for. We'll handle the rest.
What's the time commitment?
Class runs Mon / Wed / Fri from 9am–3pm (18 hours per week) for 4 weeks, plus some independent build time between sessions. The schedule is intentional — you keep earning while you build.
How selective is it?
Every applicant completes a short take-home project before a decision is made. We keep cohorts small by design — we're not looking for the most experienced people, we're looking for the most motivated.
What do I actually walk away with?
Three deployed AI products, live and working. A real product launch at Demo Day — in front of Austin operators and investors. And a cohort of fellow builders who become your long-term network — people who are building with AI in your city, in your field.
Is this right for me if I'm not trying to start a company?
Absolutely. Many builders come in wanting to automate work at their existing job, build a tool for a client, or just develop a serious new skill. You don't need a startup idea — you just need a problem you care about solving.
Is $5,999 worth it? What's the ROI?
The people who get the most out of MakerSquare use what they build to either automate expensive work at their current job (often saving 10+ hours a week), raise their market value significantly, or ship something they've been sitting on for years. A single AI-powered workflow that replaces a $50K/yr contractor pays for the program in months. That said — we don't guarantee outcomes, and the ROI depends entirely on what you do with what you build.
Why Austin?
Austin has one of the fastest-growing concentrations of founders, operators, and domain experts in the country — with an active VC and angel community that becomes part of your network the moment you launch at Demo Day.
