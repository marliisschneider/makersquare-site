Who qualifies.

Military

Active duty service members, veterans, and reservists across all branches of the U.S. armed forces.

Details

What's covered. What's not.

✓ What the scholarship covers

✓ $1,500 off the $5,999 standard tuition, bringing it to $4,499
✓ Available on all payment plan options (discount applies to total)
✓ Stackable with employer reimbursement
✓ Full access to everything in the standard program

— What it doesn't cover

— API costs during the program (~$15-30, paid separately)
— Housing (partner discounts available)
— Cannot be combined with other discount codes
— Subject to seat availability in the cohort
