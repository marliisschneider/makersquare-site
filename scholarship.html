Service Scholarship

You've served your community.
We want to invest in yours.

MakerSquare offers a $1,500 tuition discount for active military, veterans, first responders, and educators. If you've spent your career serving others, AI skills can multiply the impact you have — and we want to make that easier to access.

$1,500 off tuition.
No strings attached.

The service scholarship is a straightforward discount applied to your tuition at enrollment. There's no separate application process beyond verifying your eligibility — just submit the form below, we verify, and the discount is applied before your first payment is due.

FAQ

Questions about
the scholarship.

Can I apply for the scholarship and the program at the same time?
Yes — and we encourage it. Submit this form and the standard program application simultaneously. Once we verify your eligibility, the discount is applied to your enrollment before your first payment is due.
I'm retired — do I still qualify?
Yes. Retired military, retired first responders, and former teachers all qualify. Service is service, regardless of when it ended.
What if I'm not sure I qualify?
Submit the form and let us decide. If you've served in a public service role and aren't sure if it qualifies, tell us what you did and we'll make a fair call. We'd rather err on the side of including people.
Can the discount be combined with a payment plan?
Yes. The $1,500 discount is applied to the total tuition first ($8,000 → $6,500), and then you can spread that $6,500 across any of our payment plan options.
Can my employer also reimburse tuition on top of this?
Absolutely. Many students get partial or full employer reimbursement for professional development. The scholarship discount and employer reimbursement stack — you'd pay $6,500 out of pocket and your employer could cover some or all of that.
