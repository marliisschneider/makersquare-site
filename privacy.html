Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

MakerSquare LLC ("MakerSquare," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website at makersquare.ai. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to it. By using our website or submitting an application, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name and email address (via waitlist and contact forms)
  • Application information including professional background, location, and responses to application questions
  • Scholarship application information
  • Corporate inquiry details including company name and team size
  • Communications you send to us via email

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website:

  • Page views, session duration, and navigation behavior (via Google Analytics)
  • Browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location
  • Referring URLs and search terms

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and evaluate your application to MakerSquare
  • Send you information about the program, cohort dates, and curriculum if you join our waitlist
  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about your application status
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors engage with our content
  • Send occasional program updates and announcements (you may unsubscribe at any time)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Zoho Forms — our form provider, used to collect waitlist signups, applications, and inquiries
  • Zoho SalesIQ — used for live chat and visitor engagement on our website
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — used to measure website traffic and usage
  • Google Workspace — used for business email and internal operations

4. Data Retention

We retain application and inquiry data for up to 2 years following your submission. Waitlist data is retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard GA4 retention settings (14 months by default).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Request that we stop processing your data for marketing purposes

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@makersquare.ai.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics to track usage and by Zoho SalesIQ for chat functionality. These are analytics and functional cookies only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and programs are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
