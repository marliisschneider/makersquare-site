Founder & CEO
Marliis Schneider

Marliis has spent years in the operational trenches. Running teams, building systems, and watching firsthand as AI tools went from novelty to necessity in real businesses.

She built MakerSquare because she kept meeting smart, experienced operators who desperately wanted to use AI but had nowhere to actually learn it in depth. Her background spans operations, business development, and startup ecosystems.

Deeply embedded in Austin's tech and founder community. At MakerSquare, she sets the program vision, owns the curriculum direction, and runs the relationships that make Demo Day matter for graduates.
