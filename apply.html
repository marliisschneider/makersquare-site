Process

Three steps. About a week.

01

Fill out the application

Who you are, what you do, what you want to build, why now. No resume required.

~10 minutes
02

Complete a take-home project

Designed around what you already know. This is the most important part of your application.

2–4 hours, your pace
03

Admissions interview

A 30-minute conversation, not a test. Decision within 48 hours.

30-min video call
What we look for

Not the most experienced. The most motivated.

What matters

  • ✓

    Domain expertise

    Years in a specific industry. Your expertise is your unfair advantage.

  • ✓

    A specific problem to solve

    Vague answers here are the most common reason for rejection.

  • ✓

    Willingness to show your work

    The take-home project matters more than anything else in your application.

  • ✓

    Full commitment to the schedule

    Mon-Fri, 9am-3pm for 2 weeks, plus optional Saturday office hours.

  • —

  • —

    Prior AI experience

  • —

    A tech degree

  • —

    A fully formed startup idea

  • —

    Age, career stage, or job title
